EPL at a Glance

COVID-19 continued to have a massive impact on the world and football. Most of the premier league season was played without fans in the stadiums. During the season it looked like the away team had a better chance of a win than the home team. Manchester City ran away with the league as their squad depth proved vital to the team's winning season. Despite losing many players to injury, Liverpool ended in third place on the season's final day. Many teams surprised this season and some were able to finish above traditionally high finishing teams like Arsenal, Tottenham, and Everton.

Some of the underperforming teams this season were:

  • Sheffield United (20th)
  • Wolves (13th)
  • Southampton (15th)
  • Arsenal (8th)
  • Tottenham (7th)
  • Everton (10th)

Some of the overachieving teams this season were:

  • Manchester United (2nd)
  • West Ham (6th)
  • Leeds United (9th)
  • Aston Villa (11th)
  • Newcastle United (12th)
